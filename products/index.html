How it Works

Real-time Assessment

Smart Traffic Lights assess – in real time – the number of vehicles and people on each road and control the traffic lights in a way such that the traffic moves the fastest.

AI Optimized Coordination

They work by using a camera and computer vision to measure traffic density and traffic patterns at intersections. The data from all the traffic lights is processed together, most efficient traffic light pattern is determined using AI. Following this, a signal is sent to the traffic light to optimise traffic flow.

Interconnectivity

Smart traffic lights coordinate the operations of traffic lights to reduce vehicle idling time and provide an efficient movement of traffic.

Central Command Center

All traffic lights can be monitered from a central command center to better monitor the traffic.
